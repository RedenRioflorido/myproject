Achievements

Summary

Reden graduated from Junior High School in 2018 as Top 9 in class. Despite that he only received some fairly satisfactory grades, he managed to climbed up from 3rd Quarter due to extensive hardwork and much effort, he received very satisfactory as his final grades.

In the academic years of 2018, 2019 to 2020, Reden graduated from Senior High School, the time when he dominated his class and his fellow Technical Vocational Track (TVL) students. He took the TVL program in Information and Communication Technology with Specialization in Computer Systems Servicing where he managed to get excellent grades. During those academic years, he received a medal and two certifications from Quipper for mastering the video assessments.

During the academic year 2019-2020, he participated in the Tarpaulin Banner contest and won the contest as champion in their school. He also received an Academic Excellence Awards especially with Honors and High Honors award. Additionally, he also received Best in Work Immersion award. Lastly, also during that academic year, Reden finished a training program offered by TESDA. He received a National Certificate II in Computer Systems Servicing for completing the four core competencies.

In his freshman year from 2020-2021, he participated in the essay-writing contest and became a 3rd-Placer in the contest and being awarded. Also, during that year he was awarded a President's List award and became one of the top performing students.
