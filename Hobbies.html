Favorite Games

Cities: Skylines

Since I really love to explore and learn about Geography, Cities: Skylines is a city simulation game that I'm playing since 2021. This game taught me how to deal multi-tiered and challenging situations that exists in the game even if it is only a simulation.

This game is so much fun to play because it allows you to build your imaginary and perfect city that you can manage its urban management, policies and its basic components of a good city.

The Sims 4

Another game that I love playing with is The Sims 4 which allows you to build your own house or any establishments you want using your own design. The game does not require you to play it accordingly. The game revolves how you want to do with the life of your Sims.

Of course, I play this game by designing my own dream house with perfect family. This game is also a simulation but almost look like real because the game is associated with the real world.
